Executives are codifying **succession swim lanes** that pair mission-critical systems with the deputies who understand them. The practice shortens onboarding time for interim leaders and surfaces documentation gaps before a crisis emerges.

> Every initiative now maintains a "minimum leadership kit" containing system diagrams, vendor contacts, and decision guardrails.

When CFOs co-own the kits with HR, refresh cycles align with quarterly business reviews, making the process easier to enforce.
